# 📋 MeetDrop - User Stories (Project Backlog)

This document outlines the core requirements of the MeetDrop platform from the user's perspective. Each story defines a specific capability, the target user, and the value it brings to the networking experience.

| ID | Role | Action | Goal / Benefit | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **US-01** | New User | Sign up and log in securely (Email/Password or Google Auth) | To create and manage my digital identity within the platform. | High |
| **US-02** | Registered User | Edit my digital business card (Photo, Role, Company, Social Links) | To ensure people I meet receive my most up-to-date professional information. | High |
| **US-03** | User | Share my profile via a unique QR code or direct URL | To instantly exchange contact details without physical contact. | High |
| **US-04** | User | Save profiles I meet into a "Connection History" | To avoid losing contact information after conferences, meetups, or business meetings. | High |
| **US-05** | User | Mark specific contacts from my history as "Favorites" | To quickly access my most important connections without searching. | Medium |
| **US-06** | User | Search and filter my saved contacts by name or role | To easily find a specific person even after gathering hundreds of contacts. | Medium |
| **US-07** | User | Receive visual feedback (e.g., Toast notifications) after successful actions | To confirm that my data was correctly saved to the MongoDB database. | Low |
| **US-08** | Administrator | View a dashboard showing total registered users and activity | To monitor platform growth and overall user engagement. | Medium |
| **US-09** | Administrator | Delete fake accounts or users violating terms of service | To keep the platform professional, spam-free, and reliable for all users. | Medium |
| **US-10** | User | Be automatically logged out after a long period of inactivity | To protect my personal data and connections if my device is lost or stolen. | High |
